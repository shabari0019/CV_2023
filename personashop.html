Personashop

  • ➦ User Registration with Photo Analysis: Personashop offers a user-friendly login and signup page where users provide their name, email, and password. The unique feature involves users uploading a photo during registration for the system to calculate their age and gender.

  • ➦ Personalized Clothing Recommendations: Leveraging image analysis, Personashop provides personalized clothing recommendations based on the user's age and gender. The system tailors suggestions by analyzing the preferences and buying patterns of other users within the same age group.

  • ➦ Data-Driven Fashion Choices: Personashop utilizes data on user behavior to enhance its recommendations. By analyzing what users of similar age groups are buying more frequently, the platform ensures that the suggested clothing items are aligned with the current fashion trends within the user's demographic.

  • ➦ Location-Based Recommendations: During the initial loading process, the platform prompts users to provide their location. This information is used to refine clothing recommendations based on local weather, events, or fashion trends specific to the user's geographical area.

  • ➦ Community-Centric Shopping Experience: By integrating age, gender, location, and sales metrics, Personashop creates a community-centric shopping experience. Users not only receive personalized recommendations but also become part of a dynamic fashion community that influences and shapes the platform's offerings.
