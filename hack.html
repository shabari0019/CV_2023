Hack-n-Lead

  • Hack n Lead, a website crafted by me, serves as the digital gateway to our department's esteemed hackathon initiative. This platform was developed in the aftermath of our successful campus-based hackathon, providing an online extension to reach a broader audience. The website showcases detailed information about the hackathon, spanning various domains such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Internet of Things, and Web Development. It acts as a repository of knowledge about the event, offering insights into its objectives, challenges, and participating teams. Additionally, the website divulges the location where the hackathon is conducted, providing a glimpse into the vibrant atmosphere of innovation and collaboration. Through an intuitive design, it aims to engage participants beyond the campus, fostering a community passionate about technology and problem-solving
